Frontend Team Persona
Position What is effective? What is not effective?
School Coach The information on the website is straight forward. I can access the program results of all years. The forms to my school's region are easy to find. I do not know where I can find the schedule and the address of the upcoming league. It is confusing that League Information appears on both League Info and Program Info parts. Student The results are updated on time. I do not understand the player statistics. When I land on the page of playoff results, the result of all region in all years appear. I need to land onto the high school team scoreboard before finding the result of Junior Varsity league.
